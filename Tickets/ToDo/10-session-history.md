# 10 - Session History

## Description
Show a log of past sessions so users can see who drove and who paid on each date. This provides transparency and helps settle disputes.

## Tasks
- Create a GET `/api/sessions` endpoint that returns past sessions
- Display a history list or table on the page
- Show the date, driver, and payer for each session
- Order sessions by date (most recent first)

## Done when
- Past sessions are displayed on the page
- Each entry shows the date, who drove, and who paid
- The history is sorted with the newest session at the top
